Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

Q: How quickly are transactions processed?

All transactions on our platform are processed in real-time, typically within 2-3 seconds. Mobile recharges and bill payments are instant. In rare cases of network delays, transactions complete within 60 seconds and you receive an SMS confirmation.

Q: What happens if a transaction fails?

In case of a failed transaction, the amount is automatically refunded to your wallet within 24 hours. Our success rate is 99.9%, so failures are extremely rare. You can also raise a dispute through our support portal for immediate resolution.

Q: How many operators are supported for mobile recharge?

We support all major telecom operators in India including Jio, Airtel, Vi (Vodafone-Idea), BSNL, and MTNL. We cover all circles across India — that's 50+ operator-circle combinations. We also support international roaming recharges for select operators.

Q: Is there a minimum wallet balance required to start?

You can start with a minimum wallet load of ₹500. There is no maximum limit. We recommend maintaining a balance of ₹5,000-₹10,000 for smooth daily operations. Wallet top-up is instant via UPI, NEFT, IMPS, or bank transfer.
