Dashboard Features

Everything you need to manage and grow your business efficiently

📊

Real-time Analytics

Monitor your business performance with live charts and graphs. Track daily, weekly, and monthly earnings. Identify peak hours and top-performing services to maximize your revenue.

📋

Transaction History

Access complete transaction history with advanced filters by date, service type, amount, and status. Search any transaction in seconds. Export data for accounting and tax purposes.

💰

Wallet Management

Manage your wallet balance with ease. Add funds via UPI, NEFT, IMPS, or bank transfer. Set auto-reload thresholds so your wallet never runs dry during peak business hours.

📈

Commission Tracker

Track every rupee you earn in real-time. View commission breakdowns by service, date, and transaction. Monthly commission statements are auto-generated for your records.

📥

Report Download

Download detailed reports in PDF, Excel, or CSV format. Generate GST-compliant invoices, commission statements, and transaction summaries with a single click.

📱

Multi-device Access

Access your dashboard from any device — smartphone, tablet, or desktop. Our responsive web app works seamlessly on all screen sizes. No app download required, works in any browser.

Getting Started

Your dashboard is ready in minutes — here's how to get going

Step 1: Register

Create your free account with your mobile number and basic KYC details. Verification takes under 10 minutes.

✅

Step 4: Start Earning

Begin processing transactions for your customers and watch your commissions grow every day.
